KOHLER CENTER FOR ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Who we are: We are dreamers, inventors and doers

Part of the university's Office of Research and Innovation, the Kohler Center for Entrepreneurship helps students develop an entrepreneurial mindset, develop skills and grow their networks. Programming is open to students, faculty and staff from each of Marquette's 12 colleges and schools, engaging them through: mentoring, workshops, speaker series, funding and community partnerships. So whether you're in need of advice or have skills to share, we can help you make meaningful connections, and propel you toward growth and success.

`

Student Startups

Since the inception of the program, we have invested in numerous student-run startup spanning a number of business categories including a coffeeshop, a documentary studio, and an APP company.
`

Student Speakers

Each semester, the Kohler Center hosts conferences inviting student entrepreneurs and industrial veterans to share their experience, inspire the community, and bring in more innovative product ideas.
